HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective

Complete all 6 rescue missions across Europe to become the ultimate Rescue Pilot!

🗺️ Map Navigation

Use the interactive map to see all airports. Click on any airport within range to fly there.

⛽ Fuel

Your plane has limited fuel (2,500 km). Each flight uses fuel equal to the distance traveled. If you run out of fuel mid-flight, you crash!

⭐ Trust Points

Start with 100 Trust Points. Earn more by completing missions. Spend 20 Trust Points to refuel (+1,000 km).

🚨 Missions

When you land at an airport with a mission, you can attempt a rescue. Success depends on the disaster's severity level.

⚡ Events

Random ground events can help or hurt you. Watch out for Fatal Events - they end your game immediately!
