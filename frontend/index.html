HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective

Complete 6 rescue missions across Europe to become the ultimate Rescue Pilot!

🗺️ Map Navigation

Use the interactive map to see all airports. Click on any airport within range to fly there.

⛽ Fuel

Your plane has limited fuel (2,500 km). Each flight uses fuel equal to the distance. Run out mid-flight = crash!

⭐ Trust Points

Start with 100. Earn more by completing missions. Spend 20 to refuel (+1,000 km).

🎲 Mission Success

Success depends on difficulty. A dice roll determines outcome - you get +1 bonus to your roll!

💡 Pro Tips

  • Plan your route to maximize mission completion
  • Keep at least 500 km fuel reserve
  • Easy missions (1-3) are almost guaranteed success
  • Hard missions (7+) give better rewards but risky
  • Watch for positive events that give free fuel!
